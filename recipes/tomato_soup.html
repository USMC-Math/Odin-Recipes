Tomato Soup Recipe

This is an easy recipe to follow by those who wish to have a healthy soup that can be enjoyed by itself for light lunch or with another food item to make it a great combination.

Ingredient List and Utensils

Ingredient List

  • 4 Tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 1 Yellow union
  • 3 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Crushed tomato can 28oz
  • 1 can of Chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup of thin cut Basil
  • 1 Tbsp of Sugar
  • 1/2 Tbsp of Black pepper
  • Heavy cream to liking
  • Parmesan cheese to liking

Utensils

  • A non reactive pot
  • Immersion blender or blender

Steps

  1. We are first gonna heat up our non-reactive pot over medium heat.
  2. Once heated throw in our butter then saute the yellow cut up onions until golden and softened (10 minutes)
  3. Add minced garlic and saute for another minutes.
  4. Stir in our 28 oz cans of crushed tomatoes along with the juices, can of chicken broth, chopped basil (save some basil for garnish), sugar, black pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat. Partially covering and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. We will then blend our pot with either an immersion blender in the pot or blend in batches using a blender. Warning:(if you are using a blender be careful not to overfill with hot liquid)
  7. Next stir in the amount of heavy cream and parmesan (save some parmesan for garnish) and simmer and season to liking.
  8. You are ready to serve in a bowl with some parmesan and basil as garnish.
