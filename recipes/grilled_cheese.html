Grilled Cheese Recipe

This is an easy recipe to follow by those who wish to have a cheesey delicious lunch. It is easy to make with little ingredients and super rewarding.

Ingredient List and Utensils

Ingredient List

  • 1/2 Tbsp of butter or Substitutional: 1/2 Tbsp of mayonaise
  • 2 loafs of bread of your choosing
  • Your choice and quantity of sliced or shredded cheese Warning: The more cheese you add the more likely you'll need to finish in an oven or use a cooking torch.

Utensils

  • A pan or skillet
  • (If you are using butter)A microwave and a cooking brush
  • A spatula or tongs
  • (optional) A cooking torch or an oven

Steps

  1. First let's heat up our pan or skillet on medium heat so that it'll be ready to go while we set up our sandwhich.
  2. After we're gonna spread our butter on the outside of our loafs by melting the butter in a microwave and then spreading it with a cooking brush or a spoon. If you are using a mayonaise you can just apply a thin layer with spoon.
  3. Next we're gonna sandwhich our choice of cheese in between our loafs of bread, remembering that the outside of sandwhich should have a spread of mayonaise or butter, and we're gonna put it in our heated up pan
  4. Now we're gonna flip our sandwhich when we have a nice golden brown toast and do the same to the other side. Our sandwhich is done if we have a desired toast on our sandwhich and our cheese is melted. (if you have a desire toast and the cheese inside isn't melted) we can melt the cheese using a cooking torch.
  5. You are now ready to serve by having it sliced or non-sliced how ever you prefered.
