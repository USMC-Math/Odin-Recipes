Grilled Cheese Recipe

This is an easy recipe to follow by those who whish to have a simple yet delicous lunch, especially for those cheese lovers

Ingredient List and Utensils

Ingredient List

  • 1 Tbsp of mayonaise or alternatively butter
  • 2 Loafs of bread
  • Your choice of cheese

Utensils

  • A Skillet or Alternativly: A pan
  • A cooking torch

Steps

  1. Preheat the skillet or pan on medium heat
  2. First we want to put or Tbs of mayonaise or melted butter on the outside of our loafs of bread
  3. We are then gonna put our choice of cheese and quantity inbetween our loafs
  4. Then we are gonna put out sandwhich on our skillet and flip to the other side
  5. Finally once we have a nice golden brown on the outside and melted cheese we can pull our sandwhich off the skillet and onto a plate ready to eat
  6. If your cheese is still not melted in the sandwhich you can finish the cooking process with a cooking torch
